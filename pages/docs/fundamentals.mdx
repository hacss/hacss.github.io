### CSS in HTML
Every professional web developer is familiar with inline styles. Most prefer to
limit their use to trivial prototyping exercises only, subscribing to the notion
that markup and presentation styles address separate concerns and should not be
mixed in a production codebase. On the other hand, many find that, in practice,
these often change together, making colocation a more efficient strategy aside
from the
[functional limitations of inline styles](https://stackoverflow.com/questions/131653/inline-style-to-act-as-hover-in-css).
